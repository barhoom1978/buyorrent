Overview

Buying or renting a property is a question many people grapple with. This calculator attempts to shed some light on the financial implications of choosing the one over the other.

The following assumptions underlies this calculator.

  • no buying or renting transaction has taken place yet
  • the residence to be bought or to be rented are identical or near identical
  • the comparison is only financial, it does not consider practical implications eg, a landlord who do not wish to renew a rental agreement
  • the monthly cashflow for the renting option and the buying option is equalised, this means that the difference between what you would pay to rent (ie, monthly rent) or buy (ie, monthly mortgage) is invested in the stock market; the renterâ€™s deposit is also assumed to be invested in the stock market
  • the calculator looks at the difference in outcomes over 10 years
